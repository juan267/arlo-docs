As a farm **owner**, your role in Arlo focuses on strategic oversight: ensuring operations progress as planned, identifying significant deviations, and making high-level decisions.

---

## Your Role in Arlo

### What you should do

- Review key metrics regularly
- Monitor significant variances
- Approve budgets and important decisions
- Ensure team uses the platform correctly

### What you DON'T need to do

- Log daily operational data
- Configure sanitation forms
- Create field cycles
- Register inventory entries

Those tasks belong to administrators and supervisors.

## KPIs That Matter

### Financial

| KPI | What it tells you | Worry if... |
|-----|-------------------|-------------|
| **Cost variance** | Actual vs. budget | > 10% over |
| **Cost per kg** | Operational efficiency | Increasing trend |
| **Inventory value** | Tied capital | Growing without reason |

### Operational

| KPI | What it tells you | Worry if... |
|-----|-------------------|-------------|
| **% harvest achievement** | Productivity | < 90% of target |
| **Sanitation coverage** | Prevention | < 80% weekly |
| **Tasks completed** | Execution | < 90% of cycle |

### Risk

| KPI | What it tells you | Worry if... |
|-----|-------------------|-------------|
| **Critical alerts** | Serious problems | Any > 0 |
| **Pest trend** | Sanitary pressure | Increasing |
| **Critical supply stock** | Continuity | Below minimum |
