**Executive reports** condense your farm's information into professional formats to share with stakeholders who need high-level visibility without getting into operational details.

---

## Who Are Executive Reports For?

| Audience | Interests |
|----------|-----------|
| **Owners** | ROI, costs, yields, overall status |
| **Investors** | Financial metrics, projections, risks |
| **Partners** | Compliance, certifications, quality |
| **Consultants** | Technical data, trends, recommendations |
| **Banks/Insurers** | Inventories, production, history |

---

## Report Types

### Monthly Status Report
Summary for owners and management including executive summary, key metrics, and variances.

### Production Report
Focused on yields for planning and projections, showing production by lot and trends.

### Financial Report
For investors and accounting with operational costs, inventory, and financial indicators.

## Export and Share

### PDF Format
Professional document ready to print or send.

### Excel Format
For additional analysis or integration.

### Email
1. Generate the report
2. Click **Send by Email**
3. Enter recipients
4. Add custom message
5. Report attaches automatically

### Scheduled Sending
Configure automatic sends:

```
Schedule send:
  Report: Monthly Status
  Frequency: Monthly
  Day: 5th of each month
  Time: 8:00 AM
  Recipients: management@farm.com, owner@email.com
```

## Best Practices

### Recommended frequency

| Audience | Frequency | Content |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Owner | Monthly | Overall status, financial |
| Investor | Quarterly | Financial, projections |
| Operations | Weekly | Operational detail |
| Certifier | As audited | Traceability, compliance |

### Maintain consistency
- Use same format each period
- Makes comparing between periods easier
- Readers know what to expect

### Be honest
- Include both good and bad
- Explain negative deviations
- Show action plan for problems
