## Sanitation Module

The Sanitation module helps you systematically monitor pests, diseases, and crop health across your farm with offline-capable data collection and powerful analytics.

### Setup & Configuration

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Monitors" icon="star">
    Register and manage the field workers responsible for performing
    inspections. Track accountability and assign routes to specific people.
    [Learn more →](/en/sanitation/monitors/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Forms" icon="document">
    Create custom inspection forms with flexible sampling modes, tree sections,
    and custom fields. Use AI to help build forms based on best practices.
    [Learn more →](/en/sanitation/forms/guide/)
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

### Field Work

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Calendar" icon="seti:clock">
    Schedule, track, and manage all your sanitation inspections with a visual
    timeline. Monitor route statuses and identify overdue work. [Learn more
    →](/en/sanitation/calendar/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Route Launcher" icon="rocket">
    Start and manage field inspections with offline-first capabilities. Download
    forms before heading to the field and sync data when back online. [Learn
    more →](/en/sanitation/launcher/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Field Data Entry" icon="pencil">
    Mobile-optimized forms for collecting sanitation data in the field. GPS
    tracking, offline support, and intuitive navigation. [Learn more
    →](/en/sanitation/data-entry/)
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

### Reports & Analysis

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Route Analysis" icon="list-format">
    Review individual route results with detailed findings, GPS maps, and photo
    galleries. [Learn more →](/en/sanitation/route-analysis/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Map Reports" icon="magnifier">
    Visualize your inspection data spatially with interactive maps showing pest
    hotspots and coverage. [Learn more →](/en/sanitation/reports/map-reports/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analytical Reports" icon="puzzle">
    Deep dive into your data with advanced charts, operation metrics, coverage
    matrices, and AI-powered insights. [Learn more
    →](/en/sanitation/reports/analytical-reports/)
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
